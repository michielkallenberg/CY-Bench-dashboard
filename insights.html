Research Q1 — Which models perform best across countries?

Ranked by sample-weighted mean NRMSE (region-year level). NRMSE normalizes RMSE by mean absolute yield, so values are more comparable across countries than raw RMSE. Lower is better. Each row aggregates all crop×country datasets for that model.

Detail per crop × country × model

Δ NRMSE = mid-season − end-of-season. Positive ⇒ end-of-season is better (lower error). EOS win % = share of pairs where eos NRMSE < mid NRMSE.
